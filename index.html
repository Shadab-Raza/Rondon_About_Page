Who we are

Rondo Adult Day Services

We are a senior care service provider based in Minneapolis, US, having a vision of delivering quality services to our clients across the city.

We believe in building a stronger bond and relationship with our clientele because we understand the problem of families having an adult at home and how difficult it is to manage their overalls care and leave them alone at home in this era of rushed and busy days and times. Rondo is the best solution to the situations people are facing these days.

Mission & Target

We intend to render our elders an environment of love and care with the adequate social and mental support they crave with growing age.

We also empathise with the caregivers who find it tough to manage time after the back-breaking days to care for their elderly family members.

Hence we aim to resolve both hitches that we have successfully been resolving for 25 years and target for the distant future.
